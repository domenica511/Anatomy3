Anatomy: A Love Story

Interactive web application for studying the translation of realia in a novel by Dana Schwartz

Project Description

This resource has been created for the systematic study of realia in the literary translation "Anatomy: A Love Story" of Dana Schwartz’s historical novel. You will learn about the classification of realia, translation strategies, complete exercises and take tests.

Realia

Culture-specific elements that do not have direct equivalents in the target language.

Translation

The process of conveying meaning with regard to cultural context and style.

Strategies

Domestication and foreignization are the two main approaches to the translation of realia.
